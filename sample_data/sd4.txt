The city is very busy. Cars drive fast down the street. Honk, honk! The cars make so much noise. I look up and see a huge skyscraper. The skyscraper is made of glass and steel. "Wow, that is tall!" my brother says. City life is different from the country. We see people walking, talking, and laughing. So many people! Do they ever sleep? The sun goes down, and the city lights turn on. The lights are bright, very bright. We love walking in the City at night. It makes us happy.
