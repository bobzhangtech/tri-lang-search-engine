Cooking is fun for me. I am cooking soup today. The soup is hot, very hot! I use a wooden spatula to stir the soup. Stir, stir, stir. "Is it ready yet?" my sister asks. Not yet, I tell her. We need to add some salt and water. Water helps make the soup thin. The Sun comes through the kitchen window. I love the sun in the morning. Good food makes a happy family. We eat the soup and smile. Cooking, eating, and smiling—what a great day!
